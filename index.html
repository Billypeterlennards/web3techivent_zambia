About Web3TechEvents-Zambia

At Web3TechEvents_Zambia.com, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the Web 3, ICP and Blockchain technology community in Zambia. We are a collaborative effort between leading institutions such as the Motley Institute of Business & Technology, Information & Communication University, and DMI St. Eugene University, along with support from our esteemed partners. Meet the dedicated team behind the website who are passionate about driving the growth and advancement of Web 3 technology in Zambia.
